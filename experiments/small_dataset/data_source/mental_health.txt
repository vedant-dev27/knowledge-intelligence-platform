Mental health refers to emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how
individuals think, feel, and behave in daily life. Good mental health enables people to
handle stress, maintain relationships, and make decisions effectively. However, mental
health challenges such as anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders are
increasingly common worldwide.  Factors affecting mental health include genetics, life
experiences, work pressure, and social environments. Modern lifestyles often involve high
expectations and constant connectivity, which can contribute to mental fatigue.
Recognizing the importance of mental health is essential for maintaining overall well-
being.  Practices such as regular exercise, balanced nutrition, adequate sleep, and
mindfulness can improve mental resilience. Talking to trusted friends, family members, or
professionals provides support during difficult times. Schools and workplaces are
increasingly promoting mental health awareness and offering resources to help individuals
cope with challenges.  Reducing stigma around mental health remains a major goal.
Encouraging open conversations and providing accessible care can make a significant
difference. Mental health is as important as physical health, and taking steps to care for
the mind leads to a healthier and more fulfilling life.
